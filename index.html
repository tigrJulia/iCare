Health & wellness, right at your fingertips

Together, our focus is on making healthcare accessible, affordable, and convenient for everyone to improve positive health outcomes for all, by giving you direct access to U.S. Board Certified health providers right from your smartphone or from a computer for convenient care at home or on-the-go.

Just some perks for Members:

  • Access to U.S. board-certified & state-licensed doctors and pharmacists
  • Our providers diagnose, treat, and prescribe medication when needed
  • Quality care from wherever you are
  • Easier and better service
  • More healthcare options
  • No insurance needed

From every day to specialty care, we’ve got you covered.

We cover all of these...

Adult Everyday Care

Connect with a primary care provider for non-emergency conditions like cold & flu, allergies, and more.

Pediatric Care

Connect with a pediatrician for non-emergency conditions like cold & flu, allergies, pink eye, stomachache and more.

Dermatology

Don't wait weeks for a dermatology appointment. Start a virtual consult anytime with one of our U.S. board-certified dermatologists. With iCare+, you'll get a diagnosis, customized treatment plan and prescriptions if needed.

Women’s Health

Connect with a provider regarding birth control, urinary tract infections, menopause, and more.

Men’s Health

Consult a provider regarding hair loss or reproductive health issues such as urinary tract infections or erectile dysfunction – we’ve got you covered.

Mental Health

Schedule a visit with a therapist or psychiatrist for challenges like depression, anxiety, stress, not feeling like yourself and many others.

Pharmacist Care

Start a consultation with your pharmacist to discuss your medication, adverse drug reactions, lab results, and more.

Ophthalmology

Schedule a call with a provider regarding dry eye or pink eye.

Veterinary

Schedule a call for a consultation regarding your dog, cat, or other pet.
